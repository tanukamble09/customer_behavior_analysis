🛍️ Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis
📌 Project Overview

Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis is an end-to-end Data Analytics project designed to understand customer purchasing patterns and generate actionable business insights. The project combines Python, PostgreSQL, and Power BI to clean, analyze, visualize, and interpret customer shopping data.

The objective is to identify purchasing trends, customer segments, seasonal sales patterns, product performance, subscription behavior, and revenue drivers that can help businesses make informed decisions.

🎯 Objectives
Perform data cleaning and preprocessing using Python.
Conduct Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA).
Analyze customer behavior using SQL.
Build an interactive Power BI dashboard.
Generate business insights and recommendations.
Support data-driven decision-making.

💡 Key Business Insights
Clothing is the highest-selling product category.
Summer generates the highest sales.
Male customers contribute a larger share of revenue.
Loyal customers form the majority of the customer base.
Express shipping customers have slightly higher average spending.
Subscription alone does not significantly increase purchase value.
